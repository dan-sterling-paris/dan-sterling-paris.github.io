About Sterling Paris

Sterling Paris was founded by Dan Blumenau to offer a comprehensive management service to the theatre industry. Our approach is built on two decades of experience that combines a practical understanding of the creative process with a rigorous background in financial management.

Having started his career as a performer and later working with leading producers, Dan understands the intricacies of a production from the inside out. This is complemented by his experience as a Financial Analyst for Disneyland Paris, where he was responsible for multi-million euro budgets. This combination of creative and financial expertise allows us to provide robust, strategic support for productions of all scales.

Fluent in English and French, we are well-positioned to manage productions and partnerships across the UK, Europe, and internationally.
